Journey

From Curiosity to Craft

I started photography over nine years ago with a simple aim: to preserve memories with the same emotion people felt while living them.

Over time, that curiosity evolved into a full-time profession. I now specialize in wedding, portrait, and commercial photography with a style that blends editorial polish and candid storytelling.

Based in Ratnagiri, I work across Maharashtra and travel for destination shoots. My process is calm, collaborative, and focused on creating images that remain timeless, elegant, and deeply personal.
