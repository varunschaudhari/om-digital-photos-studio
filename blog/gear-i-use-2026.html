My Go-To Gear Setup for Weddings in 2026

Reliable camera, lens, and drone tools I trust for fast, emotional wedding storytelling.

Wedding days move fast. My 2026 setup is built around reliability, low-light consistency, and quick switching between emotional close-ups and environment-wide storytelling.

Core Camera & Lens Setup

  • Primary mirrorless bodies: Dual-body workflow for backup and speed.
  • Prime lenses: 35mm and 85mm for natural perspective + flattering portraits.
  • Low-light reliability: Fast apertures for indoor ceremonies and receptions.
  • Drone support: Aerial storytelling for venue context and cinematic opening shots.

Why This Works in Real Conditions

Hyderabad and nearby locations can shift quickly between heat, humidity, and unexpected rain. My equipment choices prioritize weather tolerance, autofocus reliability, and battery endurance for long event days.

This combination lets me stay flexible: intimate candids during rituals, quick portrait windows during golden hour, and clean low-light reception frames without slowing the flow.

Quick Gear Tips for Couples & Creators

  • Always keep a backup body and memory system on wedding shoots.
  • Use prime lenses for emotion-led portraits and storytelling depth.
  • Plan drone usage around legal zones and weather windows.
  • Prioritize comfort gear (straps, batteries, cards) for all-day consistency.
